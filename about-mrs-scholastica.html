About Mrs. Scholastica Okechinyere Onuoha

Mrs. Scholastica Okechinyere Onuoha (Nee Iheme), was born in December 21, 1947. Her life was a blessing. Her memory is a treasure. She is loved across boundaries. Appreciated beyond words, and missed beyond measure!!! She is survived by her husband, children, grandchildren, sister, nephews, nieces, cousins, in-laws, friends, associates, and a host of diverse humanity whom she positively and profoundly impacted on in her remarkable time on earth.
